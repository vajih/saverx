About SaveRx

Transparent, official ways to lower your prescription costs.

We connect you to manufacturer-run savings programs (copay cards, assistance) and give you a simple checklist so you can use them at the pharmacy—no gimmicks, no runaround.

Our story

This started with something personal. After being prescribed Repatha, the first pharmacy price was a shock. Weeks of calls and forms later, we finally found the official copay program—and the price dropped dramatically. It shouldn’t be that hard.

At the counter, we saw people—especially older patients—pay full price simply because they didn’t know where to look. We built SaveRx.ai so anyone can quickly find the real, official savings and understand how to activate them.

— Founder, SaveRx.ai

What we do

  • Curate up-to-date links to official manufacturer savings (copay cards, support, assistance).
  • Explain eligibility in plain language and outline the exact steps to enroll.
  • Help patients, caregivers, and clinics save time and reduce costs at checkout.

What we are (and aren’t)

We are a free guidance service. We are not a pharmacy, PBM, insurer, or prescriber.

We don’t sell data. We collect only what’s needed to assist you, with your consent.

Simple

  3. Use our checkout checklist at the pharmacy.

Compliance & privacy

SaveRx.ai provides educational guidance and links to official manufacturer programs. We do not provide medical advice and cannot intervene with insurers. Eligibility and terms are determined by the manufacturer and may change. See our Privacy Policy.

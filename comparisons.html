Ozempic vs Wegovy

Both contain semaglutide, but Wegovy is FDA-approved for weight loss while Ozempic is approved for diabetes.

Mounjaro vs Ozempic

Mounjaro is a dual GIP/GLP-1 agonist with potentially greater weight loss than Ozempic's GLP-1-only mechanism.

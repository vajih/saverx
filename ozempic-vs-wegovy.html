Choose Ozempic If:

  • You have Type 2 diabetes (FDA-approved indication)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use both savings cards?

No. You can only use one manufacturer savings program at a time. Choose the medication your doctor prescribes and use that specific savings card.

Are these the same medication?

Yes, both contain semaglutide. The difference is FDA approval: Ozempic is approved for diabetes, Wegovy for weight management. The dosing and formulation differ slightly.

What if I don't have insurance?

Both Ozempic and Wegovy offer Patient Assistance Programs (PAP) for uninsured or underinsured patients who meet income requirements. These programs can provide medication at no cost.

How do I enroll in these programs?

Click the "Get Savings" buttons above to access the official enrollment pages. You can enroll online, at your pharmacy, or through your doctor's office. Enrollment typically takes 5-10 minutes.

Key Differences

FDA Approval Status

Wegovy is FDA-approved specifically for chronic weight management in adults with obesity (BMI ≥30) or overweight (BMI ≥27) with weight-related conditions. Mounjaro is approved only for type 2 diabetes, though it's increasingly prescribed off-label for weight loss after showing superior results in trials.

Mechanism of Action

Mounjaro is a dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonist, activating two incretin pathways. Wegovy is a GLP-1-only agonist. This dual mechanism may explain Mounjaro's higher weight loss in head-to-head studies, though more research is needed.

Insurance Coverage Nuances

Many insurance plans cover Wegovy for weight loss if you meet BMI requirements. However, they may not cover Mounjaro for weight loss since it's off-label. If you have diabetes, Mounjaro is usually covered and offers higher savings. Check your formulary before assuming either is covered.

Savings Card Differences

Wegovy's savings card can reduce copay to $0 per month (up to $500 off), while Mounjaro's card brings it to $25/month (up to $650 off). If your copay is under $25, Wegovy may be cheaper. If it's over $650, Mounjaro offers more savings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Mounjaro for weight loss if I don't have diabetes?

Yes, but it's considered off-label use. Your doctor can prescribe it, but insurance may not cover it for weight loss alone. In this case, Wegovy (FDA-approved for weight loss) might be a better choice. Note: Zepbound (tirzepatide for weight loss) is now FDA-approved and may be an alternative.

Which causes more weight loss: Wegovy or Mounjaro?

Clinical trials show Mounjaro leads to slightly more weight loss (15-22% vs ~15% for Wegovy). However, individual results vary widely based on diet, exercise, and metabolism. Both are highly effective for weight management when combined with lifestyle changes.

Do both savings cards require insurance?

Yes. Both Wegovy and Mounjaro copay cards require commercial (private) insurance. Medicare and Medicaid patients are not eligible. However, both manufacturers offer Patient Assistance Programs for uninsured patients who meet income requirements.

Can I switch from Wegovy to Mounjaro?

Yes, but you'll need a new prescription and insurance approval. Some patients switch to Mounjaro seeking greater weight loss or if Wegovy isn't covered. Consult your doctor about proper dosing transitions and potential side effects when switching.

How long does enrollment take?

Both programs allow instant enrollment online. Complete the application in 5-10 minutes and receive a digital savings card immediately. Bring the card to your pharmacy or provide the card number when filling your prescription.

# SaveRx.ai Content System

## Mission

Build the most trusted, clear, and actionable resource on the internet for reducing prescription drug costs.

We do NOT just list savings.
We guide users step-by-step through understanding and applying savings.

---

## Core Content Types

### 1. Drug Pages (High Intent, Conversion)

Goal:

* Capture users searching for savings on a specific drug
* Provide the best explanation + step-by-step savings guidance
* Convert to email capture

Examples:

* /repatha-copay-card
* /ozempic-savings-guide

---

### 2. Learn Articles (SEO + Trust Layer)

Goal:

* Educate users on how prescription savings works
* Capture long-tail SEO traffic
* Feed users into drug pages and email flows

Examples:

* /learn/copay-cards-vs-patient-assistance-programs
* /learn/why-ozempic-is-expensive

---

## Tone & Style

* Clear, simple, human language
* No jargon unless explained
* Empathetic but not dramatic
* Trust-first (not salesy)
* Practical and step-by-step

---

## Content Rules

1. Always explain:

   * What this is
   * Who it’s for
   * What to do next

2. Always include:

   * Step-by-step instructions
   * Real-world context (cost, eligibility)
   * Common mistakes

3. Always link:

   * Drug pages → Learn articles
   * Learn articles → Drug pages

4. Never:

   * Give medical advice
   * Sound like an ad
   * Be vague or generic

---

## SEO Principles

* Each page targets ONE clear keyword
* Use natural language (not keyword stuffing)
* Include FAQ section
* Use internal linking heavily
* Make content “best on the internet” for that topic

---

## Conversion Principles

* Always include a clear CTA:
  “Get savings” or “Find your program”

* Capture email before sending users off-site

* Build trust before asking for action

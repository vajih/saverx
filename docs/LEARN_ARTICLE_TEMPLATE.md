# Learn Article Template (SaveRx.ai)

## URL Format

/learn/article-name

Example:
/learn/copay-cards-vs-patient-assistance-programs

---

## Structure

### 1. Title (H1)

Clear, search-friendly title

---

### 2. Hook (Opening)

* Identify user confusion/problem
* Make it relatable

---

### 3. Simple Explanation

Break down the concept simply

---

### 4. Section 1

Explain first concept clearly

---

### 5. Section 2

Explain second concept clearly

---

### 6. Comparison / Breakdown

Use table or bullets

---

### 7. Common Mistakes

What users get wrong

---

### 8. What You Should Do

Actionable steps based on situation

---

### 9. Internal Links

* Link to 2–3 drug pages
* Link to related articles

---

### 10. CTA

Guide user to:
“Find savings for your medication”

---

## Writing Notes

* Extremely clear and educational
* Avoid fluff
* Use examples
* Build trust
* Lead naturally into action

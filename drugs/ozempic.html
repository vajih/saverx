Ozempic savings FAQs

Who qualifies for the $25 offer?

Typically, patients with commercial insurance that covers Ozempic, subject to monthly maximum savings and other terms.

Are Medicare/Medicaid patients eligible?

Manufacturer copay programs generally exclude government beneficiaries. We’ll suggest other paths where possible.

What if I’m uninsured?

NovoCare lists a $499/month self-pay option processed outside insurance; it won’t count toward deductibles/out-of-pocket.

What happens after I get the savings?

NovoCare typically lets you print, download, or email the offer after eligibility is confirmed. Then show it at the pharmacy.
